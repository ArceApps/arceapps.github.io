Challenge your brain with **Sudoku+** anywhere and anytime!
The full game is free, with very few ads. The app itself can be used offline without an internet connection!

## Features

*   **Complete Tutorial**: Shows you all the tricks to solve sudokus, valid for both beginners and experts.
*   **5 Difficulty Levels**: Perfectly balanced levels: Easy, medium, hard, very hard and extreme.
*   **More than 5,000 Puzzles**: Well-formed puzzles on a 9x9 grid.
*   **Customization**: Lots of different designs, choose your favorite.
*   **Tools**:
    *   Unlimited Undo/Redo
    *   Auto-Fill Notes Option
    *   Auto-Clear Notes Option
    *   Highlight duplicates to avoid repeating numbers in a row, column and block.
*   **User Friendly**: Simple and intuitive design with no intrusive permissions.

Sudoku+ free includes advertisements to allow offering this game for free.

**Enjoy PuzzleHub**, the ultimate brain training collection featuring 10 classic and modern logic puzzles, all in one app. Perfect for all skill levels, PuzzleHub offers thousands of procedurally generated puzzles to sharpen your logical reasoning and mental speed. Play anytime, anywhere, even offline.

## Key Features

*   **10 Games in 1**: Enjoy endless variety with Hashi, Calcudoku, Minesweeper, and more.
*   **Unlimited Puzzles**: Thousands of levels with scalable difficulty from Easy to Expert.
*   **Multiple Game Modes**: Choose between Relax, Time Attack, and Daily Challenges.
*   **Improve and Learn**: Use smart hints and error checking to refine your skills.
*   **Track Your Progress**: Monitor statistics, streaks, and unlock achievements.
*   **Play Anywhere**: Optimized for touch screens and fully playable offline.
*   **Customizable**: Includes various visual themes.

PuzzleHub is more than just a pastime; it's your personal mental gym. If you enjoy challenges like Sudoku, crosswords, or logic riddles, you'll find your new addiction here. Enhance your mental agility, boost your concentration, and have fun solving unique puzzles. Perfect for all ages!

## Explore Our Game Collection

*   **Kakuro**: A crossword-style number puzzle. Fill the grid with digits to match the sums in the clues. (Cross Sums)
*   **Hitori**: An elimination puzzle. Shade cells to ensure no numbers repeat in any row or column.
*   **Shikaku**: A geometric challenge. Divide the grid into rectangular regions, each containing a number equal to its area. (Rectangles)
*   **Galaxies**: A symmetry game. Divide the grid into 180° rotationally symmetric regions, each centered on a dot. (Tentai Show)
*   **Hashi**: Build bridges to connect islands into a single network, matching the number of bridges to the number on each island. (Bridges, Hashiwokakero)
*   **Calcudoku**: A twist on Sudoku where logic meets math. Fill the grid respecting arithmetic operations in each cage. (KenKen, Mathdoku)
*   **Slitherlink**: Form a single continuous loop based on numerical clues in the grid. (Fences, Loop the Loop)
*   **Minesweeper**: The timeless deduction classic. Use numerical clues to locate and flag all hidden mines.
*   **Fillomino**: A territory-filling puzzle. Create regions of a specific size as indicated by the numbers within them. (Polyominous)
*   **Dominosa**: Uncover the layout of a full set of dominoes by pairing adjacent numbers. (Solitaire Dominoes)
